- **5 Workflows:** DISCOVER, ANGLE, HOOK, SCRIPT, ANALYZE

- **Multi-Account:** 8 platform strategies (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Bluesky, X, Custom)
