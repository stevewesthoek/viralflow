# About Viral Flow

## Mission

**Viral Flow** exists to solve one problem: **content creators, agencies, and teams lack a systematic, data-driven way to improve content quality at scale.**

Most teams have scattered tools:
- Google Sheets for topic tracking
- Notion for script ideas
- Analytics dashboards for tracking views
- Slack conversations about "what worked"

None of these talk to each other. The learnings are scattered. Scaling means chaos.

Viral Flow is the unified nervous system: take what you learned from last video → apply it to next video → automatically improve.

---

## The Core Insight

There are **proven formulas for content that works**:

1. **The Contrast Formula** — Juxtapose old belief vs. new insight. Creates immediate curiosity.
2. **Six Hook Patterns** — Curiosity gap, fear/urgency, benefit, contrarian, pattern interrupt, social proof. Research-backed. Copywriters have known this for decades.
3. **Composite Scoring** — Don't chase every trend. Score by: trend + low competition + audience fit.
4. **Agent Learning** — Don't forget what worked. Track performance → learn patterns → recommend next time.

These aren't new discoveries. They're battle-tested frameworks that creators use intuitively. **Viral Flow makes them systematic and scalable.**

---

## Why This Matters

### For Creators
You want to grow from 1 video/week to 10 videos/week without burning out or sacrificing quality. Viral Flow eliminates the manual research, guesswork, and analytics review. Spend time creating. Let the system handle the strategy.

### For Agencies
You manage multiple creator channels. Each has different audiences. Viral Flow scales: one system, multiple performance profiles, each learning independently. Post-production is outsourced. Analytics are fragmented. Content strategy is now systematic.

### For Teams
You're building a content engine. You need consistency, repeatability, and improvement over time. Viral Flow provides the scaffolding: discovery → ideation → scripting → performance tracking → learning. Plug in your team's production tools (video editing, TTS, posting automation). The strategy layer is solved.

---

## Design Philosophy

### 1. Research-Backed, Not AI-Hype
We use proven frameworks from copywriting, psychology, and content strategy. Not ML for ML's sake. Not magic. Just effective patterns that have worked for decades + your data.

### 2. Extensible, Not Opinionated
You have your own discovery sources. Your own content formats. Your own production tools. Viral Flow is a platform: bring your own sources, hooks, scripts. It adapts to your workflow, not the reverse.

### 3. Learnable, Not Black Box
Every function has clear documentation. Every learning mechanism is visible: "contrarian hooks drove 88% engagement on your channel—here's why." No hidden formulas. No "trust the algorithm."

### 4. Production-Ready, Not "Startup Vibes"
107 passing tests. TypeScript strict mode. Full coverage. Clean architecture. We treat this like production software because it is. You'll integrate it into your pipeline. It needs to be reliable.

### 5. Open Source, Not Gatekept
MIT license. You own your data. You own your workflows. You can fork, extend, and customize. Community contributions welcome. This is tool for teams who think differently.

---

## What Viral Flow Is NOT

- **Not a video editor.** We generate scripts; you shoot/edit videos with your favorite tools.
- **Not AI-dependent.** Use LLMs to enhance scripts, but core workflows use research-backed formulas.
- **Not a clickbait generator.** We focus on authentic angles that resonate with YOUR audience, not cheap tricks.
- **Not a replacement for creativity.** Viral Flow handles structure and strategy. You bring the voice, authenticity, and personality.
- **Not a magic bullet.** If your content is bad, Viral Flow won't save it. If it's good, Viral Flow makes it systematic.

## How It Started

Viral Flow grew from frustration:

1. **Content creators** told me they spent more time researching topics than creating. They'd scroll TikTok/YouTube for 30 minutes looking for inspiration.

2. **Agencies** told me they struggled with consistency. Different clients meant different audiences. They needed a system that could learn per-channel without manual tuning.

3. **Teams** told me their content production was ad-hoc. Scripts varied wildly in quality. Performance data was collected but never actioned. No feedback loop.

The solution? **Build the missing layer:** strategy that's systematic, data-driven, and scales from 1 to 100 videos/week.

Started as internal infrastructure. Worked so well that we're open-sourcing it.

## Technology Stack

- **TypeScript** (strict mode) — Type safety for production reliability
- **Jest** — 107 passing tests, 80%+ coverage
- **Node.js 18+** — Modern, stable runtime
- **Zero dependencies (core)** — Fast, lightweight, bring your own APIs
- **Extensible architecture** — Plugin system for custom sources/hooks/scripts

We deliberately avoided:
- Heavy ML libraries (not needed for core workflows)
- Opinionated frameworks (you choose your stack)
- Cloud lock-in (runs anywhere Node runs)
- Complex dependencies (keep it simple)
